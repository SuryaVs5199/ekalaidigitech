Web Development

Web development refers to the process of creating websites and web applications. It involves various tasks, including designing the visual interface, coding the functionality, and deploying the website on the internet. Web development encompasses both front-end development (client-side) and back-end development (server-side).

Static Websites

Static Websites consists of HTML, CSS, and possibly some JavaScript files that are pre-written and stored on the web server. Static websites are simple, lightweight, and easy to maintain compared to dynamic websites.

Dynamic Websites

Dynamic websites are websites that display content that is generated or customized in real-time based on user interactions, database queries, or other dynamic data sources. Unlike static websites, dynamic websites are more interactive, allowing users to submit forms, log in, view personalized content, and perform various actions.

Word-Press Websites

WordPress is a popular content management system (CMS) that allows users to create and manage websites without extensive coding knowledge. It provides a user-friendly interface and a wide range of themes, plugins, and customization options, making it a versatile platform for building websites of various types and sizes.

Web-Hosting

Web hosting refers to the service of storing and making websites accessible on the internet. It involves providing server space, bandwidth, and other resources necessary for hosting website files, databases, and other related components. Web hosting companies or providers offer various hosting plans and services to meet different website needs.

Page Maintenance & Development

Web page maintenance and development involve the ongoing tasks of updating, improving, and managing a website to ensure its functionality, performance, security, and relevance. It Includes Content Updates, backups, performance optimization, software updates and so on...

E- Commerce Sites

E-commerce websites are online platforms that facilitate the buying and selling of products or services over the internet. They provide a digital storefront for businesses to showcase and sell their products to customers worldwide.

Content-Creation

Content creation refers to the process of producing and sharing valuable, informative, and engaging content for various platforms and mediums. It involves creating written, visual, or multimedia content that attracts and engages the target audience, communicates a message, and provides value.
